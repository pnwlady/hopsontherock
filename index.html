Hops on the Rock Beer Festival

This November, on an island far, far away; but not too far... we're throwing a Beer Festival!

We are shutting down the streets to host a weekend of drinking, dancing and NovemBEER events all across Orcas Island.

Live music, local food and 20+ breweries from around the North Sound region will be in attendance for a destination beer festival like no other. Join us Saturday, November 11th from 11am-5pm.
