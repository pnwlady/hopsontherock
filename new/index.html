What is the Rock and why are we drinking Hops on it?

Us eccentric islanders like to refer to the island as 'the rock' partially, because it's made of rock (seriously, the geology is fascinating: glacial ice flow deposits from far away...), but also because while growing up it feels like a penitentary... a penitentary with beer!
